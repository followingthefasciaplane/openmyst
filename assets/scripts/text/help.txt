/say - Say chat
/yell - Yell chat
/whisper [name] - Whisper chat
/ignore [name] - Ignore player
/unignore [name] - Unignore player
/mall - Mutes all chat (/umall to revert)
/guild - Guild chat
/party - Party chat
/all - Global chat
/roll - Rolls a random number to party
/invite [name] - Invite player to party
/duel - Challenge targeted player to a duel
/yield - Forfeit or decline duel
/gcreate [name] - Create a guild
/gdisband - Disband guild
/ginvite [name] - Guild invite player
/gkick [name] - Guild kick player
/gquit - Leave guild
/pvp - Makes you hostile to players in PvP zones
